Make ideas grow.

We simplify innovation and research funding. For entrepreneurs, researchers and idea makers. For public financiers, investors, foundations and funds. Ymner is for everyone.

For fund seekers, we showcase available funding opportunities for everything from early ideas to industrial research and innovation. For financiers, we gather data that maps out the support system and enables data-driven funding decisions.

Our vision with Ymner is to make it possible for all to contribute to groundbreaking innovation and research.

Team

Oscar P. Ridell, Ph.D.

I have seen how unapproachable and complex the support system for innovation and research can be. I have also seen the vast untapped potential it holds. That is why I started Ymner. With Ymner I have set out to lower the barriers for fund seekers to find suitable funding and to map out the funding landscape to give financiers "bigger bang for the buck". I am convinced that this is how we create a vibrant and efficient support system over time.

Hermes Holm

As a Creative Director at agencies like Edelman and Weber Shandwick the support system for innovation and research has always been appealing to me. Through Ymner I have come to understand the system up close and over time, allowing ideas to emerge that you can now see in Ymner. One thing I have come to strongly believe is that finding new synergies between private and public funding is pivotal to securing a strong innovation and research climate for the future.
