Services for funding of Innovation & Research

Our vision

Ymner was created with the vision that more individuals, organizations, companies and financiers should be able to contribute to groundbreaking innovation and research. Based on this vision, this platform for innovation and research funding was created with services both for those seeking funding and for those who fund research and innovation.

Available funding

Ymner contains information about public grants, foundations and funds, investors, accelerators and incubators, etc. As a fund seeker you can access the latest opportunities for both public and private funding.

Create a user account to receive a weekly update with the latest grants that suit your interests.

437 grants available right now.
Funding insights

Ymner contains data that enables comparative analyzes between different research fields and areas of interest. Financiers are given access insights that visualizes the Swedish support system for innovation and research.

Ymner enables public and private financiers to become datadriven in their support of innovation and research.
